All About Me!

As you know, my name is Simon. I was born in Norfolk, Virginia but I primarily call Gilbert, Arizona my home (lived there for the vast majority of my life!). My main priority right now is pursuing a Computer Science/Cognitive Science B.S./B.A. at Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, Maryland. I'm particularly interested in Natural Language Processing.

Besides my educational pursuits, I have many other interests and hobbies. First off, I'm a HUGE foodie. I worked in a restaurant called Hibachi San (a Japanese-American fast-food type chain that's associated with Panda Express), where I spent a good amount of time in the kitchen preparing a variety of dishes and sushi. That experience sparked my passion for food and pushed me to keep learning to prepare new dishes of any kind!

Besides that, I'm also a musician. I've been playing piano for around 13 years (6 years of lessons in classical pieces) and violin for around eight years. I've also self-taught myself how to play cello and guitar (been working on especially the latter recently!).

Of course there's more to me than that, but I'm not keen to expose everything about myself! Let me know if you have any questions or more importantly, if you have any recipe or restaurant recommendation (in the Baltimore area)!
