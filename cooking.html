Welcome!

Hi! I’m Simon and I absolutely love food (cooking, eating, watching other people cook…anything!). I worked as both a chef and sushi chef in a restaurant called Hibachi-San (an Japanese-American fast food type restaurant) for 2 years and while in college, I was able to work with Steve Paret, a classically trained chef with Michelin Star experience, and learn some tips from him. These experiences, along with a childhood that was heavily saturated with food, sparked my passion for cooking and below are the results, hope you enjoy!
