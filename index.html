About Me

I am currently an undergraduate student at Johns Hopkins University studying Computer Science and Cognitive Science. My academics interests primarily revolve around Natural Language Processing, specifically in human language acquisition (and its application to computational models) and machine translation. I am always eager to learn more, especially in the fields listed above, so if you know of any pertinent point of contact, research paper/topic or opportunity, please let me know!

Outside of academics, I particularly enjoy baking and cooking (a wide range of cuisines and styles but primarily sushi due to my experience being a sushi chef) as well as learning new languages. In the past few years, I have picked up some elementary French and Spanish while also furthering my proficiency in my native tongues of English and Mandarin Chinese. Another passion of mine is music. I have played piano and violin for most of my life and have recently learned the guitar and cello.
